History & culture

Maritime heritage, interior forts, and community traditions.

Maritime identity

Oman’s coastal geography and Indian Ocean links shaped trade, craftsmanship, and seafaring traditions. Visitors experience this in waterfront districts, markets, and coastal routes.

Forts and interior heritage

Interior towns and forts reflect historic governance and architecture. Respect site rules and local customs.

Visitor conduct

  • Modest dress is recommended in public and required at religious sites.
  • Ask permission before photographing individuals.
  • Follow posted instructions at heritage sites.
