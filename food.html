Food & hospitality

Traditional dishes, sweets, and respectful dining customs.

Shuwa

Slow-cooked spiced meat, often prepared for celebrations.

Omani Halwa

A saffron-forward sweet served to guests.

Kahwa (Omani coffee)

Light coffee with cardamom, often served with dates.

Etiquette

  • Hospitality is central: accepting kahwa is a polite gesture.
  • Ask before photographing people in traditional settings.
  • Respect local customs in public dining and family venues.
