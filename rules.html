Travel rules & practical guidance

Always verify latest requirements through official sources.

Visa and entry (official portals)

  • ROP eVisa: evisa.rop.gov.om
  • Royal Oman Police: rop.gov.om
  • Oman Airports: omanairports.co.om

Keep passport validity and accommodation details available.

Dress & etiquette

  • Modest dress is recommended in public areas.
  • Religious sites: follow posted rules; cover shoulders/legs; women may be asked to cover hair.
  • Photography: request consent before photographing individuals.
  • Public behavior: respectful conduct is expected.

Driving and transport

  • Carry water when leaving cities.
  • For mountain and desert routes, plan fuel stops and check vehicle suitability.
  • Follow speed limits and signage; drive defensively.

Official tourism reference

  • Ministry of Heritage & Tourism: mht.gov.om

For official announcements and authoritative guidance, use the ministry website.
