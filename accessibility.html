Safety & emergency

Practical guidance for heat, wadis, coastal safety, and emergency references.

Emergency references

Emergency numbers vary by context; confirm locally and follow official instructions. For official public safety and services:

  • Royal Oman Police: rop.gov.om
  • Oman Airports (travel advisories / airport guidance): omanairports.co.om

Heat and hydration

  • Carry water when leaving urban areas.
  • Use sun protection; plan outdoor activity early morning/late afternoon.
  • Do not hike alone in remote areas without preparation.

Wadi safety

  • Avoid wadis during heavy rain or weather warnings.
  • Flash flooding can occur quickly—leave immediately if conditions change.
  • Wear proper footwear; stay within safe routes.

Coastal safety

  • Respect currents; swim only in safe areas.
  • Follow local signage and guidance.
  • Do not underestimate sea conditions outside guarded beaches.
